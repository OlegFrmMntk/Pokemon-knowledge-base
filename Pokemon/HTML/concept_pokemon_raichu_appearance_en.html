Appearance

Raichu is mostly orange. He has a white abdomen, and the tips of his hind and forepaws are brown. His forelegs are chubby and short. It has brown ears of a very unusual shape, on the inside they are yellow. It has a long, thin, black tail, at the end of which is a large, yellow tassel in the form of a lightning. On the muzzle yellow cheeks, small eyes and nose. Beige heels with pink cushions. Raichu moves on both four and two legs. Females have less lightning at the end of the tail than males. In addition, his coat glows in the dark.
