Behavior

In general, this Pokemon is very cocky and arrogant. He is always ready to steal food. Meowts attract round and shiny objects. As a night Pokemon, he is known to wander the streets of the city at night and picks up everything that sparkles, including small money. When a sparkling object is detected, his eyes will shine, and the coin on his forehead will shine brightly. He shares this intrigue with Markrow, with whom he often fights for objects and loot.
