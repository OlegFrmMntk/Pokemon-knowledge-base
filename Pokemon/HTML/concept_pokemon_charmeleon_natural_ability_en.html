Natural ability

The main attacks of charmeleon fire type. Due to the fact that the flame on his tail became larger, his fire attacks became much more powerful than that of the Charmander. A Charmeleon can launch an attack such as a flamethrower. From flapping the tail of Charmeleon, the air around it glows to high temperatures. Thanks to sharp claws, he has a cut attack. In addition, it can inflict great damage by blows of the tail. One of Charmeleon's tactics is to knock down an opponent with a tail strike, and then tear it with its claws. The hidden property is Solar Power.
