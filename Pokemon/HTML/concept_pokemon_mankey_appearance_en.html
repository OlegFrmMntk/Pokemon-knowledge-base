Appearance

Mankey is a Pokemon Mankey. He has a round body, which also serves as the head. The eyes are red when the decoy is calm, they are closed, open only when the Pokemon is angry. The nose is a big patch. The ears are triangular. Paws, both front and rear, long, each with three fingers. The tail is thin and long. The decoys are beige, only the hands, feet and tail end are brown.
