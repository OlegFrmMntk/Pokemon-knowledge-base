Appearance

He looks like a fur seal. It has a white color. It looks pretty funny pokemon. He has only front paws, they look like flippers. It has a tail lifted to the top, moreover, similar to a fountain. Hind legs absent. He has a horn on his forehead, two tusks sticking out of his mouth. It belongs to the water type, but does not always live in water. In order for Ciel to gain air, one has to emerge from the water. When the Pokemon is immersed in water, its nostrils are blocked by a special partition so as not to get into the water. Its size is average, but it is quite large in weight. Siil has very thick fur that will protect even from the most severe frosts.
