Appearance

Tohepi is a Pokemon that does not seem to have hatched completely from its shell; only his head and short arms and legs emerge from the shell of his egg. The parts of his body that appear have a pale, creamy color, while the shell is predominantly white. The Togepi shell also has red and blue signs in the shape of a triangle. His head has five spikes that form a crown. The eyes are small, black, oval.
