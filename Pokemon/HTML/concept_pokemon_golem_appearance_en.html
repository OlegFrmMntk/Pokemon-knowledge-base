Appearance

Externally, this Pokemon resembles a turtle. His body is round, to which his arms and legs are attached, also his head. He has three claws on his hands and five on his feet. His weight is quite heavy, he is small in height. It is very resistant to shock. This Pokemon once a year sheds its skin, which looks like a shell. His skin becomes soft, but not for long. From environmental influences, the skin becomes rough again.
