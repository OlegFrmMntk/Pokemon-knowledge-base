Appearance

Researchers are not sure to classify the bulbasaur as a plant or animal. It is essentially a cross between a dinosaur dicinodont and a toad. Bulbasaurs have no particular difference between the sexes. But it was noticed that Bulbasaurus May (female) had a heart-shaped spot on his head, while Bulbasaur Ashe (male) did not have it. This is the third pokemon in the anime that has a heart-shaped stain on its body. The other two are spin and wooper. Bulbasaurs walk on four legs, but can stand on two. With evolution, they lose this ability because the flower on the back becomes too heavy. That is why the evolution of the Bulbasaur has a much lower speed.
