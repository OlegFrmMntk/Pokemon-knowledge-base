Appearance

Nidoran is a small purple Pokemon that looks like a rabbit. He has large ears, mustache and protruding front teeth. The back of the Pokemon is covered with large thorns, from which, in case of danger, it is able to release poison. Unlike females, Nidoran has a large horn on its forehead and dark spots on its body. The sole of the feet of the Nidoran is as dense as a hoof, with two claws suitable for digging holes. The incisors of nidoran are not suitable for battle, but they are suitable for stripping off bark.
