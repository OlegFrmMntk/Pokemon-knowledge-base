Biography

According to the novelization by Takeshi Shudo, she runs Pallet Town's only restaurant, Pallet House, which she inherited from her mother. When she was younger, she dreamed of becoming both a model and a Pok√©mon trainer, but gave up those dreams to run a restaurant after Ash was born. Long ago she divorced her husband (the reason is not shown in the anime, but in the novelization of "Pocket Monsters: The Animation" the reason is revealed) and undertook to raise her son on her own. She has never been a Pokemon trainer, but in Alola Open House! has demonstrated real skill. This is how Delia won the trust of Mr. Mayme, who still helps her in the household.
