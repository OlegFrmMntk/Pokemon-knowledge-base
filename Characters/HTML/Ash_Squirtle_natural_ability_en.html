Abilities

Water-jet - Squirtle shoots a jet of water from his mouth. Skull Punch - Squirtle pinches his head and rams the enemy. Throw - Squirtle attacks the enemy with his whole body. Bubble Ray - Squirtle fires a stream of bubbles at the enemy. Retreat - Squirtle hides in a shell and strengthens the defense. Hydraulic pump - Squirtle attacks the enemy with a powerful jet of water. Quick spin - Squirtle hides in its shell and attacks, spinning fast. Bubbles - Squirtle spits bubbles at the enemy.
