Ash's Butterfree

The first Ash Pokemon he caught in the wild. Butterfree was caught as Caterpie and turned into Methapoda. The metapod then evolved, protecting Ashe and friends from the bidril swarm. Ash caught him only by throwing a pokeball. He once traded it for a Ratikate, but returned it back. Butterfree was released to start a family life with her partner, pink Butterfree. Before winning the heart of his partner, Butterfree saved a bunch of his wild relatives from Team R (this made the pink Butterfree begin to feel for him).
