Abilities

Togepi showed his abilities unnoticed by friends, which made him special among all Pokemon. In the series “Pikachu rebelled” and “Pink World”, using Metronome, he defended them. And in the series “The Terrible Forest”, also a metronome, Togepi made team R. disappear. Misty also taught him some attacks: Hug and Charm. It was thanks to these attacks that he defeated Pikachu Ash in a duel for Totodail. In the series "Mirage and Togepi!", Mastered the ability to "Protection".
