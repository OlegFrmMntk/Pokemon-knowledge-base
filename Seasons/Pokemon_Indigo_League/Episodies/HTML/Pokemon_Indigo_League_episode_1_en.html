Ep.1: Pokemon! I choose you!

The series begins with a cutscene from the Pokemon Red / Blue games, where Nidoriino fights Gengar. The image is transformed into fighting Nidoriino and Gengar in the Pokémon League arena, Gengar defeats Nidoriino with a hypnotic attack, but the mysterious trainer recalls Nidoriino and releases Onix to continue the fight. This fight is on TV watched by Ash Ketchum. Ash is a young, but energetic, determined and full of enthusiasm and determination boy who lives in a town called Alabastia (also known as Pallet Town). He turns 10 and finally gets the right to fulfill his main dream, the dream of many children - to become a Pokemon trainer. To do this, he must come to the laboratory to Professor Oak - one of the world's most famous authorities in the study of pokemon. In Pallet Town, Professor Oak gives budding trainers one of three pokemon to choose from: Charmander, Squirtle, or Bulbasaur. On the eve of this momentous day, Ash, overwhelmed with emotions, cannot fall asleep, and when he falls asleep, he has a restless dream about how he chooses different pokemon and begins his path as a trainer. He uses his alarm clock as a pokeball and as a result he wakes up the moment of distributing pokemon to novice trainers and, coming to the laboratory, meets with the grandson of Professor Oak - Garry, who has already got the pokemon and who has got fans in the form of pretty girls. Going on his way, Garry promises to glorify Pallet Town and become the greatest pokemon master in the world. Professor Oak rebukes Ash for oversleeping the moment he has been waiting for all his life, and offers him a choice of several Pokeballs, which turn out to be empty, since the pokemon have already been taken by other traines who did not sleep this moment. Ash asks the professor to give him at least some pokemon, and he invites him to take another Pokeball. This pokemon turns out to be Pikachu, in the future Ash's most faithful friend and comrade, with him they will go through all the difficulties on their way. Pikachu does not want to return to his pokeball, he does not recognize Ash as a trainer and hits him with a discharge of electricity, and then the escorts, whom Ash's mother gathered. Walking through the woods, Ash stumbles upon Rattata first, and then Pidgey, one of the best pokemon for a beginner trainer. Pikachu refuses to help Ash and the boy decides to act on his own, as a result, he not only did not catch the Pidgey or Rattata, but also angered the whole flock of Spearow, who flew after them all the way, until the very end of the series (by the way, he will still meet this flock at the beginning second season). Escaping from them, Ash jumps off the cliff into the lake and falls for the bait of a girl named Misty, from this moment we meet another main character of the anime. Misty points out to Ash where the Pokemon Center is so that the injured Pikachu can be cured there. Ash, seeing that the Spearow is close, steals Misty's bike and leaves, promising to return it someday. A thunderstorm starts, it rains, and lightnings appear in the sky. Spearow overtakes Ash and they break their bike and fall. Ash, wanting to save Pikachu, forces him to return to the Poké Ball, and then shields him with his body from Spearow. Pikachu, surprised by Ash's behavior, saves both of them by striking a flock of Spearows with lightning, and they fly away. From that moment on, Ash and Pikachu became best friends. When Ash wakes up, he sees that the storm has calmed down, and a rainbow is lit up in the sky, towards which a pokemon is flying, on which the pokedex does not give any information. Later it turns out that it was the legendary pokemon Ho-oh, and a person who sees him will have great happiness in life. Ash, leaving the broken bike, carries the exhausted Pikachu to the Pokemon Center, while Misty tries to catch up with Ash with a desire to return the money for the broken bike.
