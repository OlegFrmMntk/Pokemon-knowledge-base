Ep.3: Ash is catching a Pokemon!

Ash throws a Pokeball at Caterpie and successfully catches it. He is overjoyed at the first pokemon caught. It turns out that Misty does not tolerate bug type pokemon. Ash, ignoring her, releases Caterpie. Misty runs away behind a tree in horror, and Ash reaches out to Caterpie to climb onto Ash's shoulder. Noticing Misty, Caterpie rushes towards her and starts rubbing against her leg. Misty is reluctant to befriend Caterpie and instead pats Pikachu on the head. Frustrated Caterpie is ready to cry. Ash calms him down. Caterpie climbs onto Ash's shoulder, Pikachu jumps off Misty's arms, and they leave without her. On the way, Pikachu draws Ash's attention to Misty following them. Misty continues to follow Ash under the pretext that this is the shortest way out of the forest. Ash settles down for the night and tells the pokemon to get a good night's sleep. In response, he hears from Misty irritated: "We can sleep if you close your big mouth!" Ash and Misty fall asleep, while Caterpie tells Pikachu that he wants to become Butterfree one day. Pikachu states that Caterpie can do it. In the morning, Misty discovers that Caterpie is sleeping next to her and wakes Ash and Pikachu with her scream. She demands from Caterpie to get out of her eyes. Frustrated, Caterpie climbs into the Poké Ball. Ash assures him that someday they will show Misty what they can do. Suddenly a wild Pidgeotto arrives. Ash immediately throws a Pokeball at him, but the Pidgeotto just flips it off with his wing. Misty tells Ash to fight and weaken him before catching the pokemon. Ash releases Caterpie in hopes of showing what they are capable of. Pidgeotto easily handles Caterpie, and Ash takes him to the Poké Ball. He then orders Pikachu to use a wave of electricity. The shock paralyzes Pidgeotto and Ash catches him. Misty immediately gives Ash a lecture that the Pokémon trainer needs to know which type of pokemon to use and apply strategy in battles. She is interrupted by Team R. They demand from Ash to give Pikachu. Ash refuses and they release their pokemon. Coffing covers Pikachu's eyes with a mud attack. Ash uses Pidgeotto, but Ekans and Coffing deal with him. Ash then throws his fists at James and flies off, receiving a click on his forehead. Jassy and James laugh spitefully, believing that they have PikaPikachuchu in their hands. Ash lets out a wounded Caterpie, hoping that he will manage. Ekans and Coffing charge. Caterpie is pressed to the ground in fear. Ash commands Caterpie to use the sticky thread. Caterpie completely wraps Coffing and Ekans' head and defeats both of them with a push. Meowth also tries to join the fight, but immediately becomes completely wrapped in thread. After taking Meowth, Team R flees. Ash rejoices at the first victory in the coaching match. He praises Caterpie for a great fight and invites Misty to change her attitude towards Caterpie. Frightened, Misty reaches out to stroke Caterpie's head when suddenly Caterpie releases the thread. Misty jumps back in fright. Ash puts Caterpie on the ground and he evolves into a Metapod. Misty wants to get out of the forest as soon as possible, but Ash wants to catch more pokemon and runs away into the forest.
