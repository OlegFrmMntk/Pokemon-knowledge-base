Junichi Masuda

Game composer, director of game development, game designer, game producer and programmer, known for his work on the Pokémon franchise. He is a member of the board of directors of Game Freak, where he has been working since 1989. Prior to starting work on the Pokémon series of games, he helped write music for the Mendel Palace and Smart Ball games. In developing the first game in this series, Pokémon Red and Green, he took part as a composer and creator of sound effects, and also took on part of the programming work.
