Takeshi Shudo

Takeshi Shudo was a Japanese screenwriter and writer. His major works include the anime Space Warrior Baldios, the Magic Princess Minki Momo series, and Pocket Monsters. He had a reputation for his witty dialogue and was known for unusual previews of the following episodes of the series, in which he was the main writer. In Pokemon, he coined the iconic Team Rocket motto. He was also a member of the Japan Writers Guild.
