Geography

The Viridian Forest is a deep and vast wooded area that stretches along Route 2, reaching Pewter City in the north and Viridian City in the south, which gives the forest its name. The trees in the area are so dense that it is difficult for light to break through them, making the forest look dark and gloomy. The path leading to each destination winds its way around the dense layers of trees, creating a giant maze that many compare to a natural maze, as many people get lost inside. The entrance to the forest seems light and airy: the trees are not so dense, and the sunlight penetrates perfectly. The trees are tall and the grass looks long and unkempt, while the trees in the distance appear shaded. Due to its dark and shady environment, the Viridian Forest contains a large number of different bug pokemon. They are not particularly powerful, but they can cause status conditions such as paralysis and poisoning. From signage and even from other trainers, it is recommended that trainers purchase status healing items such as Antidote and Anti-Paralysis before entering the forest. Pokemon such as Caterpie, Weedle, Metapod and Kakuna inhabit trees and tall grass, allowing themselves to remain hidden from predators. The Viridian Forest is also one of the places where trainers can look for wild Pikachus as well as low-level Pidgeottos, making the forest a very exclusive place to find pokemon.
