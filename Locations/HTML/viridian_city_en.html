Discription

Viridian City (also called Vertania) is a city in the western part of Kanto. This is the first city visited by a player in Generation I, Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen and Pokémon: Let's Go, Pikachu! and Let's Go, Eevee! It is also the first city with a Gym, however, according to the plot, it is closed until the player has collected seven badges. The leader is Giovanni, the head of the "Team R" criminal organization, specializing in the earthen type. In the second generation and in the remakes of HeartGold and SoulSilver, Blue becomes the Gym Leader, using various types of Pokémon. The city is connected by three roads. Further south is Route 1, which leads out of Pallet Town. To the north is Route 2, leading to Pewter City and crossing the famous Viridian Forest. To the west lies the mountainous area of ​​Route 22 leading to the Indigo Plateau. In the second generation and in the remakes of HeartGold and SoulSilver, the city is known as the "Gateway to the Indigo League".
